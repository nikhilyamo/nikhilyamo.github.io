About Us:

Mayur Products (India) has achieved a good reputation as a leading manufacturer and exporter of Builders Hardware from India. 

Since last 4 decades, MayurBrass has been known for its commitment towards quality. Due to its commitment and sincerity, it has promising relations with its clients and has seen a progressive growth in this industry.

 It has 2 sister concerns and 8 ancillary units promising best quality in market. All necessary quality assurance and project management procedures are adopted for unmatched quality and timely delivery of business orders.

It has in-house facilities for all metal finishing works, which includes polishing shop, paint shop, lacquering plant and electroplating plant with Gold, Silver, Brass, Copper, Nickel Chrome, Satin plating.
